Thank you for downloading Canvas GPA Calculator!

Let's get you started with some basics:

1. Go to your Canvas dashboard, making sure that it is in card view. If it is not, please change it and reload the page. This ensures that the calculator has all your courses saved.

2. Click on the extension icon to open the menu.

3. Click on the "Auto-Update" button to calculate all grades for your courses.

4. Reload your dashboard page, and you can now see your overall GPA! For future reference, you can update your grades by clicking the "Auto-Update" button.

Bugs/errors can be reported on the Chrome Web Store
